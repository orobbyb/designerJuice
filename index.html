Designer Juice is the brainchild of three ingenious high school students who had a vision for absofruitly delicious drinks anytime, anywhere. You can design your own juice through our main website or our app, and order. Once you do, our nearest drone hub will collect your ingredients and put them inside one of our unique blender drones. It will blend the fruits into your own designed smoothie as it flies to your location for your delivery, allowing for the freshest, most desirable juice.
